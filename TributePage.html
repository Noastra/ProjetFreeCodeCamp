Son Gohan

A God among Humans

Here's a time line of Son Gohan's appearances throughout Dragon Ball:

  • First appearance at the end of Dragon Ball arc of the Demon Picolo as a baby.
  • Second appearance when Goku goes with him at KameHouse at the start of the arc of The Sayians.
  • After that he's recurrent in the anime and manga from the arc of Frieza till the end of arc Majin Buu.
  • The most impressive Son Gohan moment will be the Super Sayian 2 transformation on the Cell arc at the Cell Game.
